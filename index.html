About BSHM

BHM was founded in 2022. Welcome to Bangalore’s ultimate hip-hop haven, where street culture meets style. Our shop offers a curated collection of streetwear essentials, including eye-catching t-shirts and versatile pants that embody the vibrant energy of the city’s hip-hop scene. From bold graphics to sleek designs, our gear is perfect for making a statement or simply vibing with the rhythm of urban life. Come through and elevate your wardrobe with pieces that blend comfort, creativity, and the spirit of Bangalore’s street culture.

What is Mobb Deep?

Mobb Deep, the influential hip-hop duo from Queensbridge, is renowned for their gritty, dark production and raw, introspective lyrics, shaping the sound of 90s East Coast rap. Their seminal album "The Infamous" is a cornerstone of the genre. It was released in 1995. (Source: Mobb Deep Discography)
